The Meaning Behind Pahtia

Pahtia Labs takes its name from the Aztec word pahtia, meaning "to heal," a foundation that guides our purpose. Our focus is to create pharmacy software that elevates safety, enchances accuracy, and strenghthens the clinical work that supports patient care every day.

Pahtia Labs bridges traditional pharmacy expertise with cutting-edge technology. Our tools are built by experienced IV pharmacy professionals who understand the critical importance of accuracy in healthcare.
